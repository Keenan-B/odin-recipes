Sweet Potato Fries

Oven roasted sweet potatoes perfect for a side dish

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • Canola oil (or other oil of choice)
  • Paprika
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Slice sweet potatoes into equally sized sticks no more than 2 cm wide
  3. In a bowl, coat sweet potatoes with canola oil
  4. Add spices to bowl and distribute evenly
  5. Layer sweet potatoes flat on baking sheet
  6. Roast for 35 minutes, or until desired crispiness
  7. For best results, flip potatoes halfway through roasting
