Roast Chicken

Whole chicken slow roasted in the oven, served with vegetables

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken (~3-5lb.)
  • 3 Large yellow onion
  • 6-8 Carrots
  • 3 Stocks Celery
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 Tbs butter
  • Thyme & Rosemary
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Rough chop carrots, onions, and celery
  3. Scatter chopped vegetables and whole cloves garlic on baking pan in a single layer
  4. Stuff chicken cavity with herbs and halved lemon
  5. Place chicken on top of vegetables
  6. Rub the outside of chicken with butter
  7. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  8. Place chicken in oven for at least 1.5 hours, or until internal temp is 165 F
