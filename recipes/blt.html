Best BLT

Simple sandwhich made with bacon, lettuce, and tomato.

Ingredients

  • Fresh baked bread from bakery
  • Heirloom tomatoes
  • Applewood smoked bacon
  • Leafy lettuce
  • Mayonaise
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Line sheet tray with parchment paper
  3. Layer bacon on sheet tray, cook in oven for 10 minutes or until desired crispiness
  4. Slice and toast bread
  5. Butter bread and apply thin layer of mayonaise
  6. Layer tomatoes on bread, add salt and pepper
  7. Pat grease from bacon and place on tomatoes
  8. Add lettuce, and finally top peice of bread
  9. Slice, and enjoy
  10. (Optional)Serve with sweet potato fries
